Signal AI: Bombora Integration Use Case

Intent Data Signal Detection
Identifying accounts showing purchase intent based on content consumption patterns
1
Intent Signal Collection
SalesIntel collects intent data from Bombora, which tracks content consumption across thousands of B2B websites. The system looks for companies consuming content related to your solution's keywords at higher rates than normal.
2
AI-Powered Signal Processing
Signal AI analyzes the raw intent data using machine learning to filter out noise and identify genuine buying signals. It correlates content consumption with company profiles, industry trends, and your ideal customer profile.
3
Prioritized Account Recommendations
Based on intent strength, account fit, and historical engagement patterns, Signal AI prioritizes accounts showing genuine buying intent and recommends specific outreach strategies for each.
