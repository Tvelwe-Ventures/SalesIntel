Finding Your Best-Fit Accounts
Identify and prioritize target accounts with the highest propensity to buy based on AI-powered analysis of multiple intent and fit signals.
1
Connect Your Data Sources
SalesIntel AI integrates with your CRM, marketing automation, and third-party data providers to create a comprehensive view of your target market.
2
Define Your Ideal Customer Profile
Use the ICP Builder to specify the firmographic, technographic, and behavioral characteristics that define your best customers. The AI engine will learn from your successful deals to refine this profile over time.
3
Analyze Account Signals
The platform continuously monitors for buying signals across multiple channels, including web activity, content consumption, social media, job postings, funding events, leadership changes, and technology investments.
4
Prioritize and Take Action
Based on fit score, intent signals, and opportunity value, the AI prioritizes accounts and recommends specific actions for each. The system integrates with your sales engagement platform to execute multi-channel outreach campaigns.

Building Personalized Account Strategies

Uncover Company Initiatives

Map Key Stakeholders

3
Generate Personalized Messaging

Execute Multi-Channel Engagement
